Our Story...

Our story begins in Tilburg. A group of 4 friends wanted to be famous streamers.
In the holiday, they created a channel named 'Elite Eternal.' They made 400 subs within
2 weeks. Crazy, right? We were feeling great about it. Until, 2023 arrived. Also
New rules for the 'Coppa' law. That's a law where you are required to say if a video is permitted
for children to watch. We didn't do this. So our channel was deleted. We tried again, but didn't work.
We kept going with other channels, until we created an esports team. "Team Starflare" That didn't give any success
for 1 month. You may think it'll take longer for a small esports channel (We had a youtube Channel) to grow, but we
posted over 3 times a week. And then, we said, Alright. We're gonna do this professional. So Here we are! Hoping to Create Memories. Hoping we can reach something. Together, as a team.
